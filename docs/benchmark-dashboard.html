Reading the numbers honestly. The dramatic gaps are measured against naive — an illustrative floor with no retry or recovery. Against the fair out-of-the-box baseline (ollama-native), minimal leads by +8 pp on both models, which is not yet statistically significant (confidence intervals overlap; more tasks land in suite-v2). Its verified edge is reliability: prompt-based tool-calling that works on models without native function-calling, and single-call turns that stop blind, guessed parallel calls.
